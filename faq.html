What is SIGSAC?

SIGSAC is the Special Interest Group for Security, Audit, and Control. It is an academic club within the Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science. Through SIGSAC, we hope to teach cadets and give them the skill set needed to thrive in the cyber domain.

That's all well and good, but where do I even begin?

Lucky for you, we have the answer for that too! However, it all depends on what you want out of SIGSAC. If you want to learn about a particular topic, we have an assortment of information and tutorials to get you started. Furthermore, if we don't have quite what you want, SIGSAC can point you in the right direction.
